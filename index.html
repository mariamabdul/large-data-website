Falcons are day time birds of prey. They have long pointed wings and a notched beak, typically catching prey by diving on it from above.

They are beautiful birds that consist of many different 37 species. There are the Peregerine Falcons species which are widely distributed falcons formerly used in falconry. Falcons are roughly divisible into three or four groups.
